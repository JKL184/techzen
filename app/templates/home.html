TECHZEN FINANCIAL

The state of the SLBs website is not user-friendly and it possesses many flaws. The overall look and feel of the site will be enhanced to allow better usability. Our system will also be scalable in terms of it primarily being a loan system for students but also, persons can use this system.

Account Management

The system shall allow the user to view their account, perform actions on their respective accounts and also allow for user authentication.

Quick and easy Loan Application

The system shall allow the user to access the loan application system, and allow them to submit requested documents onto their dashboard, as well as verify their identity via camera and ID, so that the loaning agency may utilize them.

Graphical Analytics

The system shall generate specified types of graphs based on their inputs for their loans which can be used to help keep track of their loan effectively.

Credit Score Analytics

The system shall Calculate the user's credit score after the user has made the first 3 payments which will be recorded by the system.

Loan Analytics Prioritizer

The system shall be able to advise a user of which loan they should focus on paying first based on the priority of the loan
